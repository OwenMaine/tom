About Us

Your Trusted Restaurant Equipment & Supply Partner

At TOM ATSIDES RESTAURANT SUPPLIES, INC, our journey began with a simple vision: to revolutionize the way restaurants and foodservice businesses operate. Growing up in a family passionate about culinary arts and hospitality, our founders recognized the challenges faced by chefs and restaurateurs in finding reliable, high-quality kitchen equipment. This inspired us to create a service that not only supplies excellent products but also tells a story of craftsmanship, resilience, and innovation.

Picture a bustling kitchen, where every appliance hums in rhythm with the creative energy of expert chefs. Our kitchen equipment is more than just machinery—it’s a commitment to precision, efficiency, and lasting performance. Each piece is chosen with care and designed to stand up to the rigorous demands of professional kitchens. We source our products from renowned manufacturers who share our emphasis on quality, ensuring that every oven, fryer, or refrigeration unit contributes to an unforgettable dining experience.

Our story is one of continuous improvement and heartfelt dedication. We believe in building relationships just as strong as the structures in our kitchens. Every interaction reflects our passion for excellence and our commitment to helping you create culinary masterpieces that delight your guests. At TOM ATSIDES, we are proud to be a part of your story, fueling creativity and success in every restaurant we serve.
